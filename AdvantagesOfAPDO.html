Advantages of a PDO

PDO PM WANI (Public Data Office PM-WANI)

PDO PM WANI (Public Data Office PM-WANI) is an initiative launched by the Department of Telecommunications, Government of India, to provide public Wi-Fi services across the country, especially in rural and remote areas.

Advantages of a PDO

Digital Inclusion: PDO PM WANI promotes digital inclusion by providing internet access to rural and remote areas.
Economic Development: The initiative contributes to economic development by enabling online services and opportunities.
Affordable Internet: PDO PM WANI provides affordable internet connectivity, making it accessible to a wider audience.
Public Wi-Fi: The initiative establishes public Wi-Fi hotspots, providing convenient internet access.
